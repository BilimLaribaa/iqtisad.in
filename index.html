ABOUT US

About Iqtisad:
Excellence in Islamic Finance

Iqtisad is a pioneering initiative under the Center for Islamic Economics, India, dedicated to advancing Islamic Financial Jurisprudence through education, research, and consultancy. We bridge the gap between classical Islamic economic principles and modern financial systems.

With a strong emphasis on academic excellence, ethical finance, and practical application, we provide structured learning, expert mentorship, and real-world insights into areas such as Islamic banking, financial regulations, Takaful (Islamic insurance), and business law.

Our commitment extends beyond traditional education to fostering a comprehensive understanding of Shariah-compliant financial practices. Through innovative programs, research initiatives, and industry partnerships, we empower scholars, professionals, and institutions to navigate the evolving landscape of Islamic finance while maintaining authentic compliance with Islamic principles.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Islamic Finance?

Islamic finance is a financial system that operates according to Islamic law (Shariah). It is guided by principles that prohibit interest (riba), excessive uncertainty (gharar), and gambling (maysir). Instead, it promotes profit-and-loss sharing, ethical investing, and real asset-backed transactions.

Who can enroll in Iqtisad's programs?

Our programs are open to anyone interested in learning about Islamic finance - whether you're a student, professional, or simply seeking knowledge. We welcome participants from all backgrounds, faiths, and professions who wish to understand Shariah-compliant financial practices.

How are the courses delivered?

We offer flexible learning options including in-person classes, online courses, and hybrid programs. Our courses combine theoretical knowledge with practical case studies, and are taught by experienced scholars and industry practitioners in Islamic finance.
